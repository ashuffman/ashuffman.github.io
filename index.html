Hi! I'm Bob!

Here are some photos of me that my humans took

I rescued my humans in January when they came to the Oregon Humane society. I could tell right away that they needed me. So, even thought I hate being confined, I braved the carrier and the car ride to my new home.

My humans have done a great job of creating a comfortable home for me, though, sometimes they don't listen very well. I'm still working on training them. It can be frustrating, but they try so hard.
